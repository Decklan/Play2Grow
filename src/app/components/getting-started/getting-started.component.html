Getting Started

When you contact us at Play to Grow Developmental Therapy you will be talking directly with a therapist. We will do our best to serve you or to connect you with other resources to meet your needs. Please feel free to contact us by phone or email and we will get back to you within 24-48 hours. Because busy families and busy professionals do not always have regular phone hours you may find it easier to contact us by email if you choose.
